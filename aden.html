Aden Duale

Member of Parliament for Dujis Constituency

Bio

Aden Bare Duale (Somali: Aadan Barre Ducaale) is the Majority Leader of the National Assembly of Kenya. He is of Somali ethnicity. Duale is simply the most unpopular politician in Kenya due to his radical and dictatorial bills and statutory proposals he engineers to pass in the Kenyan paliarment.

  • Majority Leader of the ruling party Jubilee

    His concerted effort to defend the government in parliament earned him his second appointment in the same role to represent the government in the National Assembly of Kenya as the leader of the majority after successfully winning the Garissa Parliamentary seat (Dujis Constituency) in the 2017 elections.

    January 2017

  • Joined the Jubilee coalition.

    He joined the Jubilee coalition with his party URP and thereafter succesfully defended his parliamentary seat Garissa county,Dujis Constituency . He was appointed to represent the government in the National Assembly of Kenya as the leader of the majority.

    July 2013

  • Fall out with ODM and formation of URP

    He had a fallout with ODM's leadership where he had served as the party National Vice Chairman in charge of Political Affairs. He cofounded the United Republican Party and is a member of the G7 Leadership, a position in which he played a key role.

    April 2011

  • Yes Team

    He played many crucial roles as the ‘YES’ Team Leader for North Eastern Province that was influential in the resulting promulgation of the new Constitution in 2010.

    January 2010

  • Presidential Appointment

    President Kibaki appoints him to serve in the grand coalition government as an Assistant Minister for Livestock, representing the Orange Democratic Movement (ODM).

    April 2008

  • Dujis member of parliament

    Elected member of parliament for Dujis for the first time.

    January 15 2008
