Martha Wangari Karua

Kenyan Politician

Bio

She is a former long-standing member of parliament for the Gichugu Constituency and an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya. She was Minister of Justice until resigning from that position in April 2009.Her passion for politics and justice have made her a ‘people’s person’ over the years.

  • Supreme court

    Karua heads to Supreme Court after Appeal judges dismiss petition against Waiguru.

    December 20 2018

  • Kirinyaga gubernatorial dispute

    High Court dismisses Karua's petition against Waiguru’s win.The decision means Ms Waiguru retains her seat as Kirinyaga governor.Ms Waiguru was declared the winner in August 2017, having garnered 161,373 votes against Ms Karua's 122, 091

    November 15 2017

  • Supreme court citing

    Narc Kenya leader Martha Karua has filed a petition at the Kerugoya High Court challenging Anne Waiguru's election as Kirinyaga Governor.Through lawyer Gitobu Imanyara, Karua said the August 8 election was not free and fair.She cited the Supreme Court ruling that nullified the election of President Uhuru Kenyatta for a second term

    September 2017

  • Kirinyaga Gubernatorial Race

    Martha Karua declares her intention to run for the Kirinyaga governor’s seat in an announcement that catches many political analysts by surprise.This is because it comes weeks after she told Mombasa residents that she would be gunning for the presidency, a position she lost to President Kenyatta in 2013.

    November 2016

  • Presidential race

    Ms Karua downplayed claims that she was planning to run for the Kirinyaga gubernatorial seat.She said her party will field candidates for all positions in the next general elections.Ms Karua insisted that it is her democratic right to gunner for the country's top seat.She said that she is working to strengthen her party as well as registering and recruiting members.

    September 2016

  • Water Minister

    As Minister for Water, she implemented the Water Act 2002, which has led to reforms and service provision in the water sector.

    April 2002

  • Jurist of the Year

    The Kenya chapter of the International Commission of Jurists awarded her the Kenya Jurist of the Year.

    April 1999

  • Human rights

    In 1991, Human Rights Watch recognised Karua as a human rights monitor.
