About Us

Unapathy is all about providing the information necessary for active citizens to make the right decisions about their representatives. We seek to consolidate information from a variety of sources in one platform so that people can access it easily.

Our Goals

Providing information on representatives is one way of having an engaged citizenry. The ultimate goal is to see citizens hold their representatives accountable based on their actions or inaction.

Affiliations

We are not politically affiliated with any political party. We just want to see responsible representation from those elected to serve us, Kenyans.
